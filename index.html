WHO WE ARE

A People-First Organisation

Registered in 2008, Amiti Social Welfare Society is a non-profit committed to preventive and promotive healthcare — especially for women, adolescents, and underserved communities in Bhopal and Sehore.

We hold 12A, 80G registrations, and CSR Form-1 certification, enabling fully tax-exempt donations and corporate partnership structures designed for transparency and measurable community ownership.

Our Vision

A gender-equitable, healthy society where women can access healthcare, live with dignity, and participate fully.

Our Mission

To strengthen preventive care, empower women with knowledge, and build sustainable women-led social enterprises.

WHO WE ARE

Our Story & Mission

Serving Bhopal & Sehore Since 2008

Established under the Madhya Pradesh Societies Registration Act, Amiti Social Welfare Society was founded with a singular, resolute objective: to bridge the vast health disparities faced by women and adolescents in under-resourced rural and urban communities.

Over the last 15 years, our operational footprint has expanded across three districts, powered by a committed volunteer network of over 200 health professionals, social workers, and local changemakers. We work hand-in-hand with municipal and state agencies to align our projects directly with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being and SDG 5: Gender Equality).

We believe that healthcare is not just a clinical service; it is a fundamental pillar of human dignity and self-determination. By centering women at the heart of our community initiatives, we cultivate lasting health ownership at the grassroots level.

A gender-equitable, healthy society where every individual — especially women and adolescent girls — can access preventive healthcare, live with dignity, and participate fully in social and economic life.

To strengthen community-based preventive healthcare, empower women with knowledge and agency, and build sustainable, women-led social enterprises in convergence with national programmes.

Our Core Values

Dignity & Equity

Ensuring respectful, high-quality attention to every individual, regardless of their gender, socio-economic background, or caste.

Grassroots Action

Deploying solutions that are built and led by local community members to foster self-reliance and trust.

Scientific Integrity

Partnering with medical professionals to ensure that all advice, screenings, and drugs distributed are clinically verified.

Complete Transparency

Sharing our financial records, audits, and annual status reports openly with our partners, stakeholders, and donors.

Our Journey Timeline

A path of consistent, trust-driven growth and commitment.

2008

Inception & Foundation

Amiti was registered under the M.P. Societies Registration Act. We launched our first primary healthcare camp in the Lal Ghati area with local volunteers.

2012

Expanding to Sehore

We conducted our 50th health camp and officially set up diagnostic channels for rural districts, focusing heavily on primary checks for adolescent girls.

2017

Sarva Mangal Bhojan Launched

Seeing the nutrition challenges of hospital patients, we launched a continuous daily meal drive for cancer patients at Bhopal's main hospital.

2020

Pandemic Response & Sanitisation

Shifted resources to COVID-19 relief. Distributed hygiene kits, ran vaccination awareness drives, and deployed emergency dry rations.

2024

15+ Years & MCA CSR Form-1

Obtained CSR Form-1 registration. Successfully screened 50,000+ patients across Bhopal, Sehore, and Rajgarh districts.
